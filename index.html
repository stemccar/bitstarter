A New Flea Market Experience

Online Flea Market for all your Vintage Needs

      Open markets are a way of life. People travel all over to buy and sell their wares to consumers looking for a deal. With work, children and other obligations many people are simply unable to spend time searching for an open market to sell their gently used treasures.

      The concept for this startup is bring the convenience of the internet to those wishing to sell or buy in a flea market environment. The seller can offer a package of items or sell items individually. The buyers can browse through booths in each "Neighborhood" looking the best deals on items that spark their interest. This site will be easier to navigate than other online sites and will be wholly dedicated to bringing buyer and seller together with no hassle.
